InfoQuest Forum

About Forum

InfoQuest forum enhances students’ technical, communication, and leadership skills. Established in 2008, the forum organizes events, workshops, and social initiatives.

Forum Objectives

  1. Provide a platform for leadership growth.
  2. Invite IT sector experts for knowledge sharing.
  3. Encourage teamwork and task execution skills.
  4. Gain experience in decision-making.
  5. Host seminars on emerging technologies.
